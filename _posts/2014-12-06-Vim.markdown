### Commands

#### Search and replace

    :%s/OLD/NEW/g

#### Go to line nunber

<linenumber>G

\'G\' alone moves the cursor to the end of file; \'gg\' to the beginning
of the file.

#### Cut/Delete a whole line

    dd

#### Paste a whole line

The data from this line is placed in a local clipboard and can be
restored with \'p\'
